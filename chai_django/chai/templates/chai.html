Heyy! Let's get familiar with the History of chaii!

The history of chai began with the ancient Indian practice of brewing medicinal herbs and spices, a tradition dating back thousands of years and rooted in Ayurvedic medicine. However, modern chai, which combines tea leaves, milk, and spices, emerged after the British East India Company established tea plantations in India in the 19th century and aggressively marketed tea drinking to the Indian population. Indian vendors adapted this new beverage by adding generous amounts of sugar and local spices, creating a distinct and popular drink that became an integral part of Indian culture and social life.
